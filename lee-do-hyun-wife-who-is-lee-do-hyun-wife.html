Lee Do-Hyun Wife: Who Is Lee Do-Hyun Wife?

Lee Do-Hyun Wife: Who Is Lee Do-Hyun Wife?: Lee Do-Hyun, officially known as Lim Dong-hyun is a South Korean actor born on April 11, 1995.

He developed a love for acting at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after actors.

Lee Do-hyun graduated from the Department of Film and Theater at Chung-Ang University and made his acting debut in the 2017 black comedy Prison Playbook.

In 2018, he was cast in the romantic television series Still 17 in a supporting role as a member of the high school’s rowing club.

That same year (2018), Lee also appeared in Clean With Passion For Now, playing the female lead’s younger brother and a promising Taekwondo athlete

In 2019, he joined the cast of the dark fantasy television series Hotel del Luna which became one of the highest-rated Korean dramas on cable television.

In 2020, Lee had his first lead role in the romantic comedy 18 Again for which he bagged the Best New Actor award in television at the 57th Baeksang Arts Awards and the 7th APAN Star Awards.

In 2021, he appeared in Beyond Evil, where he played the younger self of Shin Ha-kyun’s character, and also starred in KBS2’s Youth of May

In July 2021, Lee was confirmed to join the tvN drama Melancholia with Im Soo-jung, airing in the second half of 2021.

In 2022, Lee starred in the web series Reincarnation Romance, a short web series advertising motion sickness pills.

In December 2022, he returned to the small screen with the Netflix drama written by writer Kim Eun-sook titled The Glory, with Song Hye-kyo.

On April 1, 2023, Lee Do-Hyun hit the headlines when Yuehua Entertainment confirmed that he has been in a relationship with actress Lim Ji-yeon, whom he met while filming “The Glory”
