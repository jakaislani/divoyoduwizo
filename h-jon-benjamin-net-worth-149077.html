H Jon Benjamin Net Worth

H Jon Benjamin is an actor and comedian born on May 23, 1966 in Worcester, MA. He is best known for his starring role in the Comedy Central series Jon Benjamin Has a Van, as well as providing the voices of Sterling Archer in Archer and Bob Belcher in Bob's Burgers. He has become a beloved figure in the comedy world for his unique style and wit.

About

Actor and Comedian who first gained fame for his starring role in the Comedy Central series Jon Benjamin Has a Van. He is also well known for providing the voices of Sterling Archer in Archer and Bob Belcher in Bob's Burgers. 

Before Fame

He attended Worcester Academy. From 1999 to 2002, he provided the voices of various characters, including Ben, on Dr. Katz, Professional Therapist.

Trivia

He has appeared on Aqua Teen Hunger Force and he provides the voice for Bob in Bob's Burgers.

Family Life

He has spent much of his professional life in Los Angeles. He has a son named Judah Benjamin.

Associated With
