Is Debi Mazar Single? Relationship

Paul Reubens and Debi were romantically involved in 1993. However, the couple was only together for a short while before splitting up. The next year, on March 16, 2002, she wed Gabriele Corcos. Gabriele is a well-known chef, TV host, and businesswoman. Giulia Isabel Corcos and Evelina Maria Corcos are the couple’s two daughters.

How did Debi Mazar start her Professional Career?

In the 1981 movie Downtown 81, Debi Mazar made her acting debut as a Peppermint Lounge Dancer. However, her performance in this movie went unrecognized. She also played Sandy in the 1990 film Goodfellas.

In 1991, Mazar played the parts of Whiskey Girl, Denise, and Gina in the movies The Doors, Jungle Fever, and Little Man Tate. She made an appearance in six different movies the following year.

The names of the movies were In the Soup, Love Is Like That, Inside Monkey Zetterland, Singles, Malcolm X as Toys as Suzie, Delores, Daphne, Brenda, Peg, and Nurse Debbie. She made three movie appearances in 1993: Beethoven’s 2nd, Money for Nothing, and So I Married an Axe Murderer.

In 1998, Mazar made an appearance in the movie Marry Me or Die. In the subsequent movies Frogs for Snakes, Hush, and Life in the Fast Lane, she played the roles of Simone, Lisa, and Rosie.

She then played Debbie De Luca and Jennifer Janzen, respectively, in the 1999 movies The Insider and House on Haunted Hill. Later, Debi played Mary and Rita, respectively, in the 2000 movies More Dogs Than Bones and Held for Ransom.

Two years later, she played the roles of Six and Steena, respectively, in the movies Ten Tiny Love Stories and The Tuxedo. In the 2003 movie Who Killed the Idea? and Deception, Mazar also had roles as a Mysterious Woman and Janet Steiner. She made two movie appearances the next year, in Collateral and My Tiny Universe.
