Why was La Finest Season 3 Canceled?

While there is no definitive reason for the show’s cancellation, Newsweek pointed out a pertinent piece of information that was initially revealed by Deadline in June 2020.

Season 2 of “L.A.’s Finest” was delayed to avoid airing during the George Floyd demonstrations. Given the show’s concentration on two officers as protagonists, airing it during large protests against police violence looked tone-deaf.

This may have contributed to the show’s cancellation. After all, the popular reality program “Cops” was also canceled in June 2020 in reaction to the protests. Color of Change, a civil rights organization, supported this decision.

“This is the correct course of action, and I want to commend Paramount for being one of the first. We’d like to see more, “Rashad Robinson, the group’s executive director, told The New York Times.

“These policeman reality programs that laud cops but never reveal the extent of police brutality are not reality; they are public relations vehicles for law enforcement. Law enforcement does not require public relations. This country needs responsibility.”
