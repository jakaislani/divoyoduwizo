Death Hoax and Arrest Case Update

Ja Morant is still alive, but his death rumors have been like Fire on the internet. Here’s the fact about NBA stars.

Ja Morant is a professional basketball player from the United States of America who plays for the Memphis Grizzlies of the National Basketball Association (NBA). 

Morant was the second overall pick in the 2019 NBA draft by the Memphis Grizzlies. He immediately impacted his rookie season, earning the honors of NBA Rookie of the Year and leading the Grizzlies to the playoffs.

Furthermore, Ja is known for his impressive athleticism, playmaking ability, and highlight-reel dunks and is considered one of the most promising young talents in the NBA.

Meanwhile, Morant has been involved in some legal issues. Currently, other rumors regarding Morant’s death have been circulating like a Fire. 

Ja Morant Is Still Alive: Death Hoax Debunked

As said earlier, Ja Morant’s death has drawn huge people’s attention. However, he is still alive. The death rumors have been in the spotlight for a long time, and all of them are fake.

A prank site named channel45news once said that Morant was found dead in his downtown Memphis home. They also mentioned that Morant died due to an undetected fatal illness. 

With that, fans began searching for the fact, and some of them even began paying tribute to the NBA star. The prank site lastly mentioned that they pranked the readers by writing, “You’ve been Pranked.”

So, it can be said that Morant’s death news turned fake. In the same way, the NBA player himself has not responded to anything related to these unwanted rumors. Besides that, he is now engaged in another controversy leaving people thinking he may be sentenced.

The incident has caused concern and has the potential to tarnish the young athlete’s reputation, which underscores the importance of responsible behavior in the public eye.
