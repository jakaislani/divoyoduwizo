A Professional Life

Jay Electronica started rapping at the age of 10, but it wasn’t until 2007 that he became renowned after publishing his debut mixtape, “Act I: Eternal Sunshine (The Pledge)” on MySpace. The album opened at #12 on the Billboard 200 in the United States and received positive reviews. Act II: The Patents of Nobility (The Turn), his most recent album, was released on October 5, 2020.
