Cooper Kupp contract, salary and net worth explored

Cooper Kupp is a 29-year-old American football wide receiver for the National Football League’s Los Angeles Rams.

The Rams drafted him in the third round of the 2017 NFL Draft.

Table of Content

Contract

Kupp has signed a 3-year $80 million contract with the Los Angeles Rams.

The deal carries a guaranteed sum of $35 million, a signing bonus of $20 million, a cap hit of $17 million, and a dead cap value of $47.2 million.

Salary per year

He gets paid about $26.7 million per annum. Kupp, along with Robert Woods and offseason signing Brandin Cooks, was named a starting wide receiver for the Los Angeles Rams in 2018. He caught nine catches for 162 yards and two touchdowns, including a career-high 70-yard scoring reception against the Minnesota Vikings.

A week later, Kupp was taken off the field after suffering a knee injury against the Denver Broncos.

Net worth

He is currently worth $18 million. Kupp was able to play in the Rams’ 2019 season opener against the Carolina Panthers after returning from injury. He managed to record seven receptions for 220 yards and a score in a 24-10 Week 8 win over the Cincinnati Bengals.

It was the first time in his NFL career that he had more than 200 yards in a game. Kupp’s performance set an NFL record for most receiving yards in a game played in another country. He had a receiving touchdown in five straight games.
