Linda Thompson (American songwriter) Songs, 2023, Songwriting Partners, Young, Today, Birthday, Inst

Linda Thompson is an American songwriter, former actress, and beauty pageant winner.

Linda Thompson Songs

Linda Thompson has collaborated with several stars including composer Richard Marx on Josh Groban’s first hit record, “To Where You Are”, composer Steve Dorff on the Celine Dion hit “Miracle”, Andreas Carlsson for “Drowning” by the Backstreet Boys, and composer David Foster on many compositions, such as “No Explanation” for the film Pretty Woman (1990), and “I Have Nothing” for the film The Bodyguard (1992), for which they were nominated both for the Academy Award for Best Song in 1993, and the Grammy Award for Best Song Written Specifically for a Motion Picture or for Television in 1994, and “Grown-Up Christmas List”.

Linda Thompson 2023

Linda Thompson still writes and currently resides quietly with her two dogs in Malibu California and enjoys tennis, hiking, the beach, and most of all, her family.

Linda Thompson Songwriting Partners

Linda Thompson has collaborated with several stars including composer Richard Marx, Steve Dorff, Andreas Carlsson, David Foster, and others.

Linda Thompson Young

Linda Thompson was born on May 23, 1950, in Memphis, Tennessee, United States.

At age 20, Thompson was named “Miss Mid-South Fair” in Memphis.

In 1970, she was named “Miss Memphis State University” and was third runner-up in the 1970 Miss Tennessee pageant.

Thompson was Miss Tennessee Universe in 1972, which is also known as Miss Tennessee USA and under the Miss USA and Miss Universe umbrella.

Linda Thompson Birthday

Linda Thompson celebrates her birthday every May 23.
