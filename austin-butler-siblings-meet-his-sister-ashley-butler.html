Austin Butler siblings: Meet his sister Ashley Butler

Austin Butler was born in Anaheim, California. He is the son of Lori Anne, an aesthetician, and David Butler. The two divorced when he was seven.

Austin Butler is an American actor. He began his career on television, first in roles on the Disney Channel and Nickelodeon and later on teen dramas, including recurring parts on The CW’s Life Unexpected (2010–2011) and Switched at Birth (2011–2012).

His parents and sister assisted him in beginning his career in the show business. He discovered he liked it, so he quickly started taking acting classes. Butler attended a public school until the seventh grade, after which he quit to attend home school in order to fit his schedule around his job.

Does Austin Butler movie roles

He gained recognition for starring in The Carrie Diaries (2013–2014) and The Shannara Chronicles (2016–2017). In addition to playing Tex Watson in Quentin Tarantino’s Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, Butler made his Broadway debut in the 2018 production of The Iceman Cometh (2019).
