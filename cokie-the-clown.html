Cokie The Clown

Cokie the Clown is the alter-ego and side project of NOFX frontman Fat Mike. In 2009, the eponymous EP was released through Fat Mike’s record label Fat Wreck Chords. In 2019, Cokie the Clown released his first full-length album You’re Welcome.
